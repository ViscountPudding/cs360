Poem

I am Incorruptible
Rule-maker, the head taker,
A man without error with virtue and terror
As my twin-edged blade, which can't fade
Even while my sharp sword cleaves in spades
The monarchists and their apologists who stirred up my rage,
Opened the cage,
Let revolution take center stage,
Where the only resolution could be blood on the history page.
The loyalists and royalists had better show their respect
To Miss Lunette, who calmly puts her hands on their necks
So Madame Guillotine can finish up and call for the next
Citizen who disgraces that name
This isn't a game, a chance for fortune and fame
The poor-exploitng trash that would put reason to shame,
But rather a hope for the greatest republic for mankind,
Don't get left behind, As
Even former friends can wind up supping in Hades
With Antoinette and here ladies. There will be
No flip-flopping, I'm not stopping
The drop of the blade, chopping
Through the necks of my enemies,
Those who would try to concot cruel conspiracy
Those who would foolishly try to fight liberty
End up the targets of Freedom's true tyranny
